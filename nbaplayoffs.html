The NBA Playoffs - and what to make of them



















A couple of things have set these playoffs apart from past years so far:

  1. The unusual lack of upsets (until Game 2's began)
    • In the 14 games played so far, only 2 upsets have happened
  2. The great defense that's being played
    • As you probably know - the NBA isn't exactly known for its defense
      • This is especially true when it comes to star players...
        • Luka Doncic - in specific - has been a known defensive liability for his team for some time
        • That being said, he held opponents to 2/17 when he was the primary defender in Game 2
        • This was a marked anomaly from his past performances, and very impressive given that he scored 33 points in that same game


















But...who will actually win this year?

I'll give you 3 answers, and reasons for each

  1. The Celtics
    • They are indeed the odds-on favorite
    • But they have choked quite often, which is why I think it'll be....
  2. The Nuggets
    • They are the defending champs after all!
      • They are locking down the Lakers right now, which is pretty difficult to do.
  3. The Thunder?
    • Wait who let them on this list?
      • Think about it - they did win the West
      • You gotta respect 'em at least a bit for doing that. They have an outside shot, but it's certainly there
